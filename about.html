// COMPANY OVERVIEW

CARVAN is the largest online car marketplace designed to cater to the needs of families, businesses, and individual buyers. Our platform provides complete information, detailed specifications, and high-quality images of various car models offered by different automobile companies.

The website is structured to display car brands along with their available models. It also categorizes vehicles into Small Cars, Sedans, Vans, Jeeps, and other segments to make browsing simple and user-friendly.

Categorized by vehicle class—from urban compacts to heavy-duty off-roaders—our interface is designed for high-speed navigation and informed decision making.
